--- layout: post title: Idempotent tags: - Tumble status: publish type: post published: true meta: dsq_thread_id: '17413903' --- UPDATE: It seems Network Solutions has turned off their search "feature" where they register the domain you just searched. Here is a fun little experiment...
  1. Do a search for a random domain on GoDaddy.com. I chose networksolutionsactuallyregisterseverydomainsearched.com. If the domain has been taken, think of another one.
  2. Then go to Network Solutions and do the search on the same domain. Note that the domain is still available.
  3. Now go back to GoDaddy.com and search one more time.
Hmmm. Seems Network Solutions searches are not idempotent.
